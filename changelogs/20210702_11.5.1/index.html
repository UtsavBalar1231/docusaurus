2021.07.02 v11.5.1

Source side Changes#

  • Added Amoled dark mode.
  • Fixed charging source recognizing when using non oem chargers.
  • Improved haptics logic in calculator application.

Device side Changes#

  • Switched to full OTA builds for easy flashing and upgrade.
  • Fixed a bug in firmware where audio used to cut after alarm ended on type-c headphones.
  • Fixed a bug in audio hardware where it would cut audio for 500ms~ after switching to apps that support voIP.
  • Fixed a problem where USB PD authentification woud not happen causing thermal mitigation to not switch settings and resulting in slower charging.
  • Fixed a problem where the stock type-c to jack adapter woud display analog is not supported.
  • Improved panel response time/rate and addressed color differences between 60/120 hz.
  • Init display with 120hz as default refresh rate.
  • Lowered minimum Display brightness level and increased maximum auto brightness value.
  • Lowered Fingerprint sync time to around 450ms~when in doze mode.
  • Fixed panel LTM modes not applying in AOD Power Save and HBM states.
  • Lowered recording volume on google voice recorder.
  • Retuned powerhal boosting configuration for better performance and idle.
  • Fixed OpenCL not being utilized on Genshin Impact and AIDA64 not recognizing OpenCL Device.
